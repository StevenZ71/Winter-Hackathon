The Problem

Calculus can feel like a blizzard of confusion. Cat-culus warms things up by turning learning into a game!

Gameplay Overview

  • Build an 8-card deck of calculus topics (e.g., Product Rule).
  • Use mana to play cards; solve problems to cast spells!
  • Correct answers trigger charming spell animations; mistakes cost mana.
  • Mana and cards replenish naturally over time.
  • Failed cards return early, testing your quick recovery!
